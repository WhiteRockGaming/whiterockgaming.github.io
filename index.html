modpack: Less Tech More Dragon

How do I download a modpack?

  1. Use the Twitch app - twitch.tv/downloads
  2. Open Twitch
  3. go to "Mods" tab
  4. click Minecraft
  5. search for "dragon" or "animals"
  6. Click on the thing you want
  7. click Install
  8. wait for Install
  9. click Play
  10. profit.
